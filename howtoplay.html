How to Play Slither Quest

Welcome to the Slither Quest! Here's how to play:

  1. The objective of this game is to maneuver a snake around the game board, consuming food items to grow longer without colliding with the walls of the game board or the snake's own tail.
  2. Use the arrow keys (← ↑ → ↓) or the on-screen controls to control the snake.
  3. Eat the red food to grow the snake and earn points.
  4. Avoid running into the walls or the snake itself.
  5. You can choose how fast the snake will move.
  6. Try to achieve the highest score possible!

Tips & Tricks!

  1. Plan ahead and anticipate the snake's movements to avoid trapping yourself.
  2. Utilize the edges of the board to maneuver and create space for the snake.
  3. Stay vigilant and focus on the food while being mindful of your surroundings.
  4. Maintain a balance between speed and control to avoid collisions.
  5. Use the snake's body as a barrier to navigate tight spaces.
  6. Practice patience and avoid rushing into risky situations.
  7. Remember to take breaks to rest your eyes and maintain concentration.
  8. Monitor the length of the snake and adjust your strategy accordingly.
  9. Stay calm and composed, even in intense situations, to make better decisions.
  10. Challenge yourself by setting personal goals and aiming for higher scores.
